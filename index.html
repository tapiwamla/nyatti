How long does it take to build a website or web application?

Website development timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple business website or landing page typically takes 1-4 weeks to design and launch. Custom web applications, ecommerce websites, and online stores can take 6-12 weeks or more depending on features like payment integration, inventory management, and custom functionality. We'll provide a detailed project timeline and milestone schedule during our initial consultation based on your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We provide comprehensive website maintenance and support services for all our projects. This includes regular security updates, software patches, performance optimization, bug fixes, content updates, and 24/7 technical support. Our affordable maintenance packages ensure your website or web application continues to run smoothly, stays secure, and performs optimally for your business.

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern, industry-leading web development technologies including Next.js, React, Node.js, Supabase, WordPress development, WooCommerce for ecommerce, and headless CMS solutions. Our developers are proficient in JavaScript, TypeScript, PHP, and modern frameworks. We select the best technology stack based on your project requirements, scalability needs, performance goals, and budget to ensure optimal speed, security, and maintainability for your business.

How much does a website or web application cost?

Website development cost varies based on project scope, features, and complexity. Simple business websites and landing pages start from affordable rates, while custom web applications, ecommerce platforms, and enterprise solutions are priced based on specific requirements like database integration, payment systems, and advanced functionality.
